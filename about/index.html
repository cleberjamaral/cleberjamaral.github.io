About

After my technitian studies in Electronics and Microprocessor Systems at Technical School of Santa Catarina (ETFSC) in 1998, I have obtained my undergraduate degree in Electronics Systems at CEFET-SC (2007) and a Master Business Administration (MBA) in Business Management at Univali (2012). Later, I have obtained my Master Degree in Mechatronics at Federal Institute of Santa Catarina (IFSC) in 2016. Currently, I am a PhD student at Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC) in the Graduate Program in Automation and Systems Engineering.

I have worked a total of 15 years on electronics industry as hardware and software designer and Manager of Research & Development (R&D) Department at my own company and later at Automatiza which was bought by Intelbras SA in 2013. Since 2016, I am Lecturer in the Telecommunications area at Federal Institute of Santa Catarina (IFSC) teaching programming languages, artificial intelligence, distribucted systems, embedded systems and telecommunication projects.

My research interests are: Organisations and Programming Tools for Multi-Agent Systems (MAS), Distributed Systems, Machine Learning and Programming Languages.
